Common questions

Quick answers to what most people ask before signing up.

How is Knox different from ChatGPT or other AI tools? +
Knox is built specifically for learning. He shows step-by-step work, explains why each step is right, and is tuned for K-12 through college subjects. General chatbots are great for trivia — Knox is great when you actually need to learn the material, hand it in, and remember it for the test.
Will I be charged after the 3-day free trial? +
Yes — unless you cancel before the trial ends. The free trial on Super Knox Monthly gives you 3 days of full access. After that, $9.99/month is charged automatically. You can cancel anytime from your account page (we'll show you how), and you'll keep access until the period you paid for ends. No surprise fees.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yep. Sign in, click your name in the top-right, then "Manage Billing" — that takes you to a secure Stripe page where one click cancels. You'll keep using Knox until the end of your current billing period, no questions asked.
What subjects does Knox cover? +
Every subject you'd see in K-12 through college: math (algebra, geometry, calculus, stats), all sciences (biology, chemistry, physics), history, English/essays, foreign languages, economics, computer science, and more. If your teacher assigns it, Knox can help with it.
Can I upload a photo of my homework? +
Absolutely — and most people do. Click the photo icon in the input box, snap a picture of your worksheet or textbook problem, and Knox reads it directly. Available on every plan including Free.
Is this just cheating? +
That's actually why Knox has two modes. Answer mode gives you the solution fast — useful when you're stuck and just need to move on. Learn mode walks you through the problem step by step like a tutor would, so you understand the material. Knox is designed to teach, not just to do your work for you.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee on yearly plans, and you can cancel any monthly plan anytime. Email support@knoxknowsapp.com and we'll take care of it. See our full refund policy.
What if Knox gets something wrong? +
Knox is highly accurate but not perfect — like any tutor. Always double-check important answers against your notes or textbook, especially for graded work. If you spot something off, hit the 👎 button below the response and we'll use that to make Knox smarter. Direct feedback also welcome at support@knoxknowsapp.com.

Still have questions? Email support@knoxknowsapp.com — Sion (a real human) reads every message.
