🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: April 2026 · Knox Knows

📋 The short version

  • ✔We only collect what we need to run Knox Knows
  • ✔We never sell your data to anyone
  • ✔Your questions stay private — not shared or sold
  • ✔You can delete your account and data at any time
  • ✔Payments handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card
1
What we collect

We collect the following information when you use Knox Knows:

  • Account info — your name and email address when you sign up via Google, Microsoft, or email
  • Usage data — daily question counts to enforce plan limits (stored for 30 days, then deleted)
  • Questions — the questions and images you submit to Knox to generate answers
  • Billing info — handled entirely by Stripe. We only store your Stripe customer ID, not card details
  • Device info — browser type and basic technical info for debugging
2
How we use your data

We use your information only to provide and improve Knox Knows:

  • To log you in and keep your account secure
  • To enforce your daily question limits based on your plan
  • To process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send you important account-related emails (receipts, password resets)
  • To improve Knox's answers and fix bugs

We do not use your questions to train AI models, sell your data, or send you marketing emails without your consent.

3
Third-party services

Knox Knows uses the following third-party services to operate. Each processes some of your data under their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database storage. Privacy policy ↗
  • OpenAI — powers Knox's AI responses. Questions are sent to OpenAI's API. Privacy policy ↗
  • Stripe — handles all payment processing. We never see or store your card details. Privacy policy ↗
  • Vercel — hosts the Knox Knows platform. Privacy policy ↗
4
Cookies & local storage

Knox Knows uses cookies and browser local storage for the following purposes:

  • Authentication — Firebase uses cookies to keep you signed in
  • Usage tracking — we store your daily question count locally so the progress bar updates instantly
  • Theme preference — we remember whether you prefer dark or light mode
  • Chat history — stored locally in your browser only, never on our servers

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

5
Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Usage records (daily question counts) are stored for 30 days and then automatically deleted.

If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance (Stripe retains billing records as required by law).

6
Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@knoxknowsapp.com

7
Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication is handled by Firebase, which uses industry-standard security practices. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our servers.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8
Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Knox Knows after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you by email or by displaying a notice on the site.

9
Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

support@knoxknowsapp.com
